## Summary

Deleted all hardcoded wave/section constants from `generation.ts` (SECTION_WAVE_MAP, SECTION_NAMES, TOTAL_SECTIONS, WaveNumber, getWaveSections, createInitialSections) and replaced them with `GenerateSectionPayloadV2` тАФ a v2 payload contract keyed by proposal_sections UUID. Rewrote `useProposalGeneration` to fetch `proposal_sections` ordered by position and loop sequentially, passing completed sections as `priorSections` to each call. Added D-04 section pre-creation to `ProposalCreationWizard`: at Generate click, the wizard fetches `template_sections` and inserts `proposal_sections` rows (status='pending') before navigating to ProposalDetail.

## Decisions Made

- `GenerateSectionPayloadV2` uses `version: 2` discriminant so the edge function can handle both old and new payloads during rollout
- `consistencyAnchor` retained as a field alongside `priorSections` as a safety net (per plan discretion)
- Fallback template UUID `00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000001` used when no template selected (D-02: "no template" state does not exist)
- `upsert` with `onConflict: 'proposal_id,section_key'` used for proposal_sections insert to be idempotent on re-runs
